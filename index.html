Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Vision and Mission of the Department


VISION

To become pioneers in Computer Education and Research and to prepare highly competent IT professionals to serve industry and society at local and global levels.

MISSION
  • To impart high quality professional education to become a leader in Computer Science and Engineering.
  • To achieve excellence in Research for contributing to the development of the society.
  • To inculcate professional and ethical behavior to serve the industry.

Introduction


Foreseeing the scope in the growth of Information Technology, Computer Science and Engineering department was established to offer Undergraduate Programme in Computer Science and Engineering way back in 1984. Initially, department started with an intake of 40 students. This department achieved phenomenal growth under the able administration.


Current intake of students for Computer Science and Engineering Department is: 120.To keep pace with technological trends in the IT sector, department is offering Graduate course (BE), two Post Graduate Programmes (M.Tech), namely, Computer Science & Engineering and Computer Network & Engineering and a Research Centre.


In the year 2013, Research Centre was sanctioned by VTU Belgaum to boost research activities in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. There are more than 50 research scholars have registerd for Ph.D. under the research center. One research scholar is awarded Ph.D. registered under CSE research center. Three more research scholars registered under CSE research center have submitted their thesis and waiting for final defence.


Department has highly qualified and experienced faculty with specializations in Image Processing , Computer Networking , Mobile computing and Data Analytics etc. There are eight faculty having Ph.D. degree. Almost all faculty members are persuing Ph.D. and most of them are on the verge of completing Ph.D. award.


Faculty of the department have published more than 250 technical publications in reputed Journals. Publications of CSE department have earned good number of citations and reference indexes like, h-index, i10-index, google scholar and scopus indexes. There are seven patents have been filed by faculty members under different domains. The average teaching experience of faculty is more than 15 years.


Department is sanctioned grants for infrastructure development under Vision Group of Science and Technology (VGST), Govt. of Karnataka of Rs. 40Lakhs to establish cloud computing lab. Also, it has received grant for quality technical publications under Awards for Research Publications(ARP) scheme under VGST.


Many student projects are selected for grants under KSCST, Govt of Karnataka and also under Govt of Karnataka initiative, New Age Incubation Network (NAIN) programme. CSE department students have participated in technical events like, Code Hackathon, Project competions and conferences organized by other technical colleges.


The department has ISTE and IETE student chapters for encouraging technical events.


Students of this department have secured ranks in final exams and bagged many awards in curricular and co-curricular activities. Students of UG and PG also have published quite a good number of technical papers in National and International reputed journals.


Department has state of the art computing facilities to cater to the needs of students. To name a few computing facilities - Wi-Fi network, Data Servers, over 150 desktops, laptops interconnected via fiber optic network to share the computing resources. Department follows software ethics strictly. All the systems are installed with licensed software like Ubuntu, Windows XP, Windows 7, Windows 8, Linux, Dot Net, Java, C/C++, Oracle, QUALNET Network Simulator and other system and application software. Department has 7 Labs, namely, Basic Computing Lab, Advanced Computing Lab, Hardware Lab, IoT and Projects Lab, PG and Research Lab, Cloud Computing Lab and Cyber Security Lab. All labs are equipped with power safety and fire safety equiments. Computing Systems are maintained by in-house maintainance group headed by HOD and Systems Manager. Department also maintains campus network which has more than 1000 systems. The main control center of Campus Network is in CSE department and it is maintained by CSE department.


Department has conducted many useful workshops and seminars and collaborative international workshop in the field of computer science and engineering.


The placement record of department is quite impressive. Its average place of students 76%. The average package of salaries of students is quite impressive.The success rate of students of CSE department is 98%.


Department got accreditation by NBA twice during 2004 and 2008 each time for 3 years. Now, department has applied for Tier-I NBA accreditation (under Washington accord).


The department has quite impressive and strong Alumni base. Many Alumni of this department are performing as entrepreneurs in India and abroad as well. Many others are well positioned and working in government and private sectors.
