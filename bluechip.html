BLUECHIP ASSOCIATION


A student's association named "BLUECHIP STUDENT ASSOCIATION" has been started earlier in 1990 to help conduct activities and other events in the college. It has an elected body where the general secrataries and other club members are elected by voting done by students and faculty. One of the faculty members is associated with the association to oversee and advise the elected secrataries for conducting the events. Our association organizes both curricular and extra-curricular activities.


Events conducted

  • Every year, Project exhibition of final semester students.
  • Conducting Seminars and Workshops hosted by department
  • Conducting Cultural activities
  • Conducting Quiz, Programming Competitions, Seminars, Lectures, Talent Shows Etc.
  • Organized Freshers Day for 3rd sem Students every year.
  • Organizing Farewell meet every year.
  • Organizing Alumni and Parent meets.
  • Organizing sports activities.
